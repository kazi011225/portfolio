CS student.

I grew up in Woodside, Queens — the kind of neighborhood that teaches you to figure things out. Before I ever touched a terminal, I spent over a decade in the automotive industry, working my way from Sales Consultant all the way to Finance Manager and Assistant General Manager.

That career gave me something most CS students don't have: real experience solving problems under pressure, communicating with every kind of person, and understanding that technology only matters if it works for real people.
